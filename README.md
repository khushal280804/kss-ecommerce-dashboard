## 📌 Overview

The **Ecommerce Sales Analysis Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence report built to provide a comprehensive view of sales performance, profitability, and customer order trends across an ecommerce retail dataset. It enables stakeholders to monitor KPIs, explore category-level breakdowns, analyze geographic distribution, and filter data dynamically by year, customer segment, and region.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Track overall business performance using key financial metrics
- Identify top-performing product categories and subcategories
- Analyze monthly sales and profit trends over time
- Understand geographic sales distribution across US states
- Enable interactive filtering for segment- and region-specific insights

## 📊 Dashboard Components

### 1. Sales and Profit Analysis (Monthly Bar + Line Chart)
- Dual-axis chart showing **monthly Sales (bar)** and **Profit (line)** trends
- Covers January through December
- Highlights seasonal peaks, particularly in **October–December**

### 2. Category Wise Profit (Waterfall Chart)
- Visual breakdown of profit contribution by category:
  - **Furniture:** ₹18.45 K
  - **Office Supplies:** ₹122.49 K
  - **Technology:** ₹145.45 K
  - **Grand Total:** ₹286.40 K
- Color-coded to show Increase, Decrease, and Total

### 3. Category Wise Sales Share % (Donut Chart)
- **Technology:** 42.77%
- **Office Supplies:** 51%
- **Furniture:** 6%
- Total Sales: ₹2.30M

### 4. Sales by State (US Map)
- Choropleth map visualizing sales intensity across all US states
- Darker shades indicate higher sales concentration
- Sales range: ₹0.92 K – ₹457.69 K

### 5. Top 5 Subcategories by Sales (Horizontal Bar Chart)
| Subcategory | Sales      |
|-------------|------------|
| Phones      | ₹330.01 K  |
| Chairs      | ₹328.45 K  |
| Storage     | ₹223.84 K  |
| Tables      | ₹206.97 K  |

### 6. Filters Panel
Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering:

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Open the `.xlsx` file in **Microsoft Excel** (2016 or later recommended)
2. Navigate to the **Dashboard** sheet to view the full report
3. Use the **Slicers** (Year, Segment, Region) to dynamically filter all visuals
4. Hover over chart elements for detailed data tooltips
5. All charts are linked to **Pivot Tables** — avoid editing raw pivot data directly
